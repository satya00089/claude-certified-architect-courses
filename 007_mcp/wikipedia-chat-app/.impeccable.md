# Design Context: Wikipedia Research Assistant

## Users & Purpose
- **Users**: Professionals doing quick research for work or writing
- **Job to accomplish**: Quickly retrieve and synthesize Wikipedia information
- **Context**: Focused, efficiency-driven work sessions

## Brand Personality
**Modern, sleek, cutting-edge** with emphasis on **professionalism and sophistication**

## Emotional Goals
- **Primary**: Calm focus (minimal stress, easy to use)
- **Secondary**: Confidence in data quality and professionalism

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Remove**: Bold purple gradients (too decorative and intense for professional use)
- **Add**: Sophisticated typography, refined spacing, premium feel
- **Keep**: Modern aesthetic, clean interface, efficient layout
- **Mode**: Light mode (professional context favors light UIs)

## Design Principles
1. **Refinement over boldness** — Elegance through typography, spacing, and subtle details
2. **Professional sophistication** — Premium feel without excessive decoration
3. **Clarity through hierarchy** — Strategic use of scale, weight, and color
4. **Calm energy** — Reduced visual intensity, intentional contrast
5. **Modern minimalism** — Less is more, but not barren

## Key Changes
- Replace gradient backgrounds with solid, sophisticated colors (neutrals with subtle tints)
- Improve typography: Better font hierarchy, refined sizing
- Enhance spacing: More precision, better rhythm
- Reduce visual noise: Remove decorative effects, simplify elements
- Add personality through: Strategic use of color accent (single, refined color), micro-interactions with purpose
